About Jon

Jon Gabrielli is one of the top rising comedians in California. A Norcal native, he performs regularly at major comedy clubs, theaters, colleges, breweries, wineries, casinos and more all over the country. Jon opens for names like Tim Dillon, Matt McCusker, TJ Miller, Pablo Francisco and many more. He has had 2 Kill Tony appearances, was featured at Sundance Film Festival, and was a semi-finalist in the San Francisco International Comedy Competition. His comedy is interactive and creative, he takes audiences on journeys through his mischievous outlook on the events in his life.
